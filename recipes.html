Recipes-World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adopted the recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Granger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's resturant in Kins Cross, Sydney, I have been after the recipe. I have since transformed into what I really believe are the best scrambled eggs I have vere tasted.

This recipe is whst I call a "very special breakfast", just look at the ingredients to see why. It has to be to tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:
  • 2 eegs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a hight heat(taking care not to burn the butter).
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan(around 20 seconds).
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
